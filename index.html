TINAGO FALLS

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Tinago Falls in Iligan City, where a majestic 240-foot cascade plunges into a serene, emerald lagoon surrounded by lush greenery. Perfect for adventure seekers and nature lovers, the falls offer a thrilling trek, refreshing swims, and picture-perfect views. Whether you're diving into the crystal-clear waters, rafting across the calm lagoon, or simply soaking in the tranquility, Tinago Falls promises an unforgettable escape into nature's paradise.
